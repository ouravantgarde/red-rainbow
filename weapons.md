## NAME NEEDED 1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

> A gun that contains a powerful form of rotational energy, shooting out chaotic bolts that have decent proc rates.
> 
> While the rotational force it contains warrants practically anchoring oneself to the ground, it can be used to create a theoretically infinite feedback loop.

%-se\[-\] **Primary Fire**

%-wst**Damage** : 100

%-wst**Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 2

%-wst**Proc Rate** : 1.25

%-wst**Proc Intensity** : 1

%-wst**Ammo Size** : Infinite

%-wst**Refresh Time** : 3



Fires a high velocity bolt. Every projectile shot past four will slow Avnlyla down by 25%, becoming **Immobilized** after 8 shots. 

This goes away when holstered and the build-up resets when the weapon refreshes.

%-se\[-\] **Alternative Fire**

%-wst**Damage** : 25

%-wst**Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 1.5

%-wst**Proc Rate** : 0.33

%-wst**Proc Intensity** : 1

%-wst**Ammo Size** : Infinite

%-wst**Refresh Time** : 8



Fires a high velocity "Spiralized Bolt". If this directly hits and damages an enemy, adds a consecutive Spiralized Bolt to the next firing. This causes an exponential growth of projectiles, although it will **Immobilize** Avnlyla in just mere seconds should they successfully hit. 

This also enforces a much longer refresh time, completely immobilizing Avnlyla even if she isn't firing.

%-se\[-\] **Notes**

Ammo Size increases will scale how many shots it takes to slow, and eventually **Immobilize**, Avnlyla.

Dashing will holster the weapon if Avnlyla is **Immobilized**, allowing her to dash away without issue. She will equip whatever has the most ammo, or whatever was last used if not applicable.

%-sr

## NAME NEEDED 1 *B-SIDE*&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

> A more advanced rotational force that can be interfaced with, which in this case allows the user to use it's acceleration instead of trying to supress it.
>
> Its actually rather unstable, but you can translate the ludicrous speeds it gives you right back into the weapon itself... If you can aim it at such speeds.

%-se\[-\] **Primary Fire**

%-wst**Damage** : 50(x3)

%-wst**Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 1.2

%-wst**Proc Rate** : 0.75

%-wst**Proc Intensity** : 1

%-wst**Ammo Size** : 30

%-wst**Refresh Time** : 5


Fires a burst of projectiles with tight spread. Should any of them hit, it will increase Avnlyla's movespeed multiplier by +10%.

This movespeed fades over 5 seconds unless another stack is gained, and only applies while held. While this weapon is held, Avnlyla is unable to stop moving, and attempting to stand still will continue movement in the previous direction moved.


%-se\[-\] **Alternative Fire**

%-wst**Damage** : 100

%-wst**Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 0.1

%-wst**Proc Rate** : 1

%-wst**Proc Intensity** : 1

%-wst**Ammo Size** : Infinite

%-wst**Refresh Time** : N/A

%-wst***Cast Time*** : 0.6

Charges up to release a large bolt of energy, **Immobilizing** very briefly once it fires.

The projectile's base damage and velocity are all scaled by Avnlyla's final movespeed. If this would result in over 2000 base damage, Avnlyla is stunned for 1.5 seconds.

%-sr
