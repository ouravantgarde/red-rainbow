## NAME NEEDED 1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

%-fdA gun that contains a powerful form of rotational energy, shooting out chaotic bolts that deal good damage with decent proc rates. While the rotational force it contains warrants practically anchoring oneself to the ground, it can be used to create a theoretically infinite feedback loop.

%-se\[-\] **Primary Fire**

%-st **Damage** : 100

%-st **Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 2

%-st **Proc Rate** : 1.25

%-st **Proc Intensity** : 1

%-st **Ammo Size** : Infinite

%-st **Refresh Time** : 3

Fires a high velocity bolt. Every projectile shot past four will slow Avnlyla down by 25%, becoming **Immobilized** after 8 shots. This goes away when holstered and the build-up resets when the weapon refreshes.

%-se\[-\] **Alternative Fire**

%-st **Damage** : 25

%-st **Fire Rate (Per Second)** : 1.5

%-st **Proc Rate** : 0.25

%-st **Proc Intensity** : 1

%-st **Ammo Size** : Infinite

%-st **Refresh Time** : 8

Fires a high velocity "Spiralized Bolt". If this directly hits and damages an enemy, adds a consecutive Spiralized Bolt to the next firing. This causes an exponential growth of projectiles, although it will **Immobilize** Avnlyla in just mere seconds should they successfully hit. This also enforces a much longer refresh time, completely immobilizing Avnlyla even if she isn't firing; It is best she puts this down for a bit after using the Alternative Fire's full potential.

%-se\[-\] **Notes**

Ammo Size increases will scale how many shots it takes to slow, and eventually **Immobilize**, Avnlyla.

Dashing will holster the weapon if Avnlyla is **Immobilized**, allowing her to dash away without issue. She will equip whatever has the most ammo, or whatever was last used if not applicable.

%-sr
